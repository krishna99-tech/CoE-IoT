About COE-IOT Lab

Leading the Digital Transformation through Industrial 4.0 and Smart Factory Innovations.

Pioneering Industrial 4.0 Research

Established in 2023, the CoE Laboratory is a premier research facility funded by ZF Commercial Vehicle Control Systems India Limited. We are dedicated to driving the Industrial 4.0 revolution by integrating advanced Internet of Things (IoT) technologies with industrial automation to create intelligent, self-optimizing manufacturing systems.

Our core expertise lies in the fundamental pillars of Smart Manufacturing: including Industrial IoT (IIoT), real-time energy monitoring, predictive maintenance, and cyber-physical systems. Through strategic industry-academia partnerships, we bridge the gap between academic research and industrial-scale deployment, solving real-world challenges in the modern factory environment.
