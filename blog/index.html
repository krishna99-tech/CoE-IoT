About COE-IOT Lab

Our state-of-the-art IoT research facility is dedicated to advancing connected technologies and smart systems.

Industry-Funded Research Excellence

Established in 2018, the COE-IOT Laboratory is a premier research facility funded by leading technology companies including Technova and Innovate+. Our mission is to drive innovation in Internet of Things technologies through collaborative industry-academia partnerships.

We focus on cutting-edge research in areas including smart cities, industrial IoT, connected healthcare, and sustainable energy systems. Our industry partnerships ensure that our research has real-world impact and addresses current market needs.

With over 30 industry projects completed and 15 patents filed, we continue to push the boundaries of IoT technology and its applications across various sectors.
